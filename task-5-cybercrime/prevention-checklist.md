### Stay Safe Online Checklist :-

UPI / Financial Safety Tips:-  
1) Do not make UPI or online payments on public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks  
2) Do not enter your pin anywhere while receving payments  
3) Regularly check your bank statements for any suspicious transactions  

General Safety Tips :-  
1) Do not click on suspicious and unkown links   
2) Do not share any personal information online  
3) Keep strong passwords by using mix of letters,numbers and special charecters  
4) Verify information and facts before beliving or using them from trusted source and sites  
5) Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts  
6) Always log out from accounts after using someone else’s device  
7) Do not allow unnecessary permissions (camera, contacts, etc.) to apps  

Reporting Help :-  
Report cyber fraud at :- https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Call Cyber Crime Helpline :- 1930 (24x7)
